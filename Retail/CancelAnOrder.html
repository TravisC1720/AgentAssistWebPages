Cancelling an Order

Canceling an order will stop a customer's current delivery.

Customer wants to adjust the delivery date instead?
1. Have them log in and go to My Menu in the top left corner of the page. Youâ€™ll see your order menu.
2. Have them use the arrow to the right of the date to go to the order in question.
3. Have them click ADJUST DELIVERY below the menu you want to skip.
4. Voila! They've adjusted the delivery date the order.

Customer wants to cancel the order
1. Have them log in and go to Plan Settings.
2. Have them scroll down to the bottom and click CANCEL ORDER.
3. Have them follow the directions given.
The cutoff is at 11:59 pm PST, 3 days before the delivery. This is so we can notify our suppliers.
